He specializes in Mechanical Engineering. Currently he is working with ITC Ltd.His interests find a long list starting from writing stories, poetry to photography, painting, trekking, travelling and music. He finds pleasure in spending time with nature. Often he can be spotted beside a river or a lake with his camera.
