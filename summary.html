Summary

It is the beginning of life on earth. Life sprouts in the navel of KAUSAR; a land blessed with contours and landscapes. As life opens its eyes in the backdrop of silence and darkness, 'Heart of Kausar' also the narrator finds herself in a dark arena. Her disappointment about the place soon turns into fear and anxiety. But before the disaster stars appear in the sky and she decides to wait.

Change in the flavor of colors on east further cheers her up. Happiness returns to her. She enjoys the Redglory celebration and appreciates the bonding between Souls of Nature. As she enjoys the introduction of benevolent shades in her life, her Mind shifts her focus to KAVI, the protagonist.

Kavi is sad. Heart of Kausar, closely follows Kavi. One night, Kavi gets up to a knock on his heart. He looks around but returns clueless. Kavi expresses his feelings in the form of poetry. Next morning he gets up early and looks for his dream but soon convinces himself that she is just an illusion of his heart.

Then one fine morning he spots her. He sees her in the temple. Temple looks divine. KAVYA, other main character, urges him. He approaches her and talks to her. After initial display of fear and puzzlement, both display shades of love.

They start spending time together. Kavi asks her to join him for boating, moseying and spotting. Love intensifies between them. Color of Red-Rainbow deepens between them. They discover the language of love and language of Souls of Nature. They discover the lovely stitch of twines of love between Sun and Moon.

Soon monsoon follows. They greet monsoon by playing in drops of love. They appreciate monsoon by engaging in copulation. Love formulates its ground in the heart of Kausar.

One fine day, their happy journey hits the stone of time and he finds himself alone in the temple.

He waits, bellows, begs, urges and asks for her. His heart breaks down. His emotions break down. His feelings break down.

He seeks help from Souls of Nature. He asks for the help of Lake, he asks for the help of Tree-of-Love and he asks for the help of sparrow.

Then one fine night she finally visits him in his dream. They talk about their misfortune. She begs him to strengthen relations with Souls of Nature. She gifts him their babies. She convinces him about the love of life and she promises to stay along his side.

Her words change his outlook. He breaks his chains of selfishness and establishes contact with every soul. Kavi at last finds love in the ties of fauna. Kavi at last finds serenity in the ties of flora. Kavi at last finds happiness in the ties of nature.


"Shades of Shadows" is a poetic and intriguing saga of love between two hearts, who meet on the canvas of nature. Prose and poetry has been lyricked together to form a perfect blend of color. Poetry has been used to describe thoughts, emotions and feelings at various stages. Poetry, at various moments, showcases hidden attributes. Total of 50 poems have been used to capture various spectrum of thoughts of souls during the course of events in the novel. Prose complements poetry.
